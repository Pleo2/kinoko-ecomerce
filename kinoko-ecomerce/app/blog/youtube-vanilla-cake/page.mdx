# How to Make the Perfect Vanilla Cake

Watch our step-by-step video guide to baking a delicious vanilla cake.

## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/3 cups granulated sugar
- 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients.
3. Add the softened butter, milk, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes.
4. Add the eggs and beat for an additional 2 minutes.
5. Pour the batter into prepared cake pans.
6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
