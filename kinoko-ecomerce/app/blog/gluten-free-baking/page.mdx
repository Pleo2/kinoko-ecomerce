## Moisture is Key

Gluten-free baked goods can dry out quickly. Learn techniques to keep them moist:

- Adding extra liquid
- Using fruit purees
- Incorporating yogurt or sour cream

## Gluten-Free Cake Recipe

Try our foolproof gluten-free vanilla cake recipe:

1. Mix 2 cups gluten-free flour blend, 1 tsp xanthan gum, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1/4 tsp salt
2. In another bowl, cream 1/2 cup butter with 1 cup sugar
3. Add 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla to the butter mixture
4. Alternately add dry ingredients and 3/4 cup milk to the butter mixture
